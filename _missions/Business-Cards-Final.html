🎯 Design Analysis & Recommendation

  • Option A (Minimalist): Best for first meetings with conservative JB dentists. Black panel creates authority, blue name adds modern touch without overwhelming. Logo isolation makes it memorable. Professional but approachable.
  • Option B (Executive): Best for CEO positioning and conference networking. Full gradient creates maximum visual impact. White text on blue = high contrast legibility. Premium feel matches "leading AI platform" narrative.
  • Back Side (Both): QR code on light blue background ensures high scan success rate. "Scan to Become a Partner" is direct call-to-action. 32mm QR size is optimal for phone cameras at arm's length.
  • My Recommendation: Option A for dentist meetings (professional, trustworthy). Option B for investor/media meetings (bold, visionary). Print 100 of each for different contexts.
