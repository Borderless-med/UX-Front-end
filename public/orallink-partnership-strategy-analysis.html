🔑 Critical Understanding

This is a strategic partnership, not just a technical integration:

  • AI scan is the bridge, not the primary goal
  • Dr Gerald's dentist network is OraChope's real prize
  • OraChope's traffic is OralLink's validation/monetization path
  • Both parties win if they help each other achieve core goals

Value Exchange (What Each Party Brings)

🦷 OraChope Provides to OralLink:

  • High-intent traffic: 10K+ users actively looking for dental care
  • Lead generation: Pre-qualified users who need AI scans
  • Distribution channel: Saves OralLink marketing budget
  • Revenue potential: If OralLink charges for scans, OraChope drives conversions
  • Proof of concept: Validates OralLink's business model with real volume

🔬 OralLink Provides to OraChope:

  • AI scan feature: Makes OraChope offering more complete/competitive
  • User acquisition: "Free AI scan" is strong marketing hook
  • Dr Gerald's network: Introductions to JB dentists (PRIMARY VALUE)
  • Professional credibility: Partnership with dental professionals
  • Differentiation: Competitors don't offer AI scan + marketplace combo

💎 The Hidden Value: Dr Gerald's Network

The AI scan integration is important, but Dr Gerald's ability to introduce JB dentists to OraChope is the strategic crown jewel. If OraChope can onboard 50-100 new JB clinics through Dr Gerald's connections, that's worth far more than the AI scan traffic itself.

🏗️ Partnership Structure Options

⭐ RECOMMENDED

Option 1: Traffic-for-Introductions Model

"You introduce us to dentists, we drive traffic to your AI scan"

How It Works:

1
OraChope drives traffic to OralLink
"Get FREE AI Scan" button in chatbot → Redirects to orallink.health with tracking
2
Track performance monthly
OraChope reports: X users sent to OralLink, Y completed scans, Z conversions
3
Dr Gerald introduces JB dentists quarterly
Based on traffic volume: 100+ scans/month = 5 dentist intros/quarter
4
Partnership tier system
More traffic = more introductions. Creates incentive alignment.

Partnership Terms Example:

Traffic Tier (Scans/Month) OralLink Commitment (Intros/Quarter) OraChope Commitment
Bronze: 50-100 scans 3 dentist introductions AI Scan button in chatbot
Silver: 100-300 scans 5 dentist introductions + Email campaign to user base
Gold: 300+ scans 10 dentist introductions + Featured placement on homepage

✓ PROS

  • Clear value exchange: Traffic for introductions, both measurable
  • Incentive alignment: More you deliver, more you get
  • Low technical complexity: Just redirect + tracking
  • Dr Gerald's core value: Leverages his JB network (his superpower)
  • Scalable: Can increase commitment as partnership grows
  • No revenue sharing: Keeps accounting simple
  • Easy to test: Start small (Bronze tier), scale if successful

✗ CONS

  • Dr Gerald's time commitment: Making introductions takes effort
  • Quality control: How to ensure intro'd dentists are good fits?
  • No guaranteed dentist onboarding: Intros don't guarantee partnerships
  • Tracking complexity: Need attribution for scans from OraChope
  • User experience gap: 40-60% abandonment after scan (dead end problem)
  • OralLink may not see value: If they don't need traffic (have other channels)

✓ Best For:

Use this model if:

  • Dr Gerald has strong JB dentist connections and is willing to make intros
  • OralLink needs traffic/lead validation more than immediate revenue
  • OraChope values dentist network expansion over AI scan feature perfection
  • Both parties want to test partnership with minimal risk

Scenario Volume OralLink Revenue OraChope Commission (20%)

Freemium (10% upgrade to paid) 500 scans, 50 paid ($15/month) $750/month $150/month
Paid scans ($10/scan) 200 paid scans/month $2,000/month $400/month

✓ PROS

  • Financial upside: OraChope earns money directly (vs just traffic)
  • Performance-based: Only pay for results (aligned incentives)
  • Scalable revenue: More traffic = more earnings automatically
  • Clear accountability: Easy to track/measure with affiliate links
  • Motivates optimization: OraChope has reason to improve conversion
  • Standard model: OralLink likely understands affiliate partnerships

✗ CONS

  • OralLink may not monetize: If scans are free, no revenue to share
  • Trust required: OraChope must trust OralLink's conversion tracking
  • Low revenue potential: If only $150-400/month, not worth effort
  • Accounting complexity: Invoicing, payments, tax implications
  • Dr Gerald's intros secondary: Money becomes focus, not network access
  • Competitive dynamic: Feels transactional, not strategic partnership

⚠️ Best For:

Use this model if:

  • OralLink actively monetizes AI scans (charges users or has subscription model)
  • OraChope needs revenue justification (not just dentist network access)
  • Both parties want measurable ROI from day 1
  • Caution: May undervalue Dr Gerald's intros if focus shifts to commission

Option 4: Dentist-First Partnership Model

"Dr Gerald introduces dentists first, AI scan integration second"

How It Works:

1
Phase 1: Dentist Introductions (Month 1-2)
Dr Gerald introduces 5-10 JB dentists to OraChope → Onboard them first
2

OraChope shows we can drive bookings to Dr Gerald's network dentists

Phase 2: AI Scan Integration (Month 3+)
After proving OraChope works, integrate AI scan as "premium feature"
4
AI scan drives to Dr Gerald's network
Users complete scan → OraChope recommends clinics (prioritizing Dr Gerald's intros)

Strategic Rationale:

🎯 Why This Works

The AI scan is more valuable AFTER you have Dr Gerald's dentists onboarded:

  • Closed loop: AI scan identifies needs → OraChope recommends Dr Gerald's network clinics → Dr Gerald sees direct benefit
  • Proof before investment: Dr Gerald takes low risk (just intros), sees results, then commits to AI scan integration
  • Stronger pitch: "We already drove 50 bookings to your dentist friends" beats "We'll drive traffic to your AI scan"
  • Network effects: More Dr Gerald dentists = better OraChope offerings = more valuable AI scan integration

✓ PROS

  • Gets the prize first: Dentist network access before technical complexity
  • Low risk for both: Intros cost nothing, prove value before AI scan work
  • Dr Gerald's motivation clear: OraChope drives bookings to his network
  • Builds trust gradually: Small steps, not big commitments upfront
  • Creates leverage: If Phase 1 succeeds, Phase 2 easier to negotiate
  • Skips dead end problem: No AI scan integration means no 40-60% abandonment initially

✗ CONS

  • Delayed AI scan: OraChope doesn't get "complete offering" immediately
  • Dr Gerald may not commit: If intros don't convert, partnership stalls
  • No OralLink traffic: OralLink gets nothing in Phase 1 (may not agree)
  • Requires patience: Partnership value realized over months, not weeks
  • Intro'd dentists may underperform: Network quality unknown until tested

📋 Phase 1 Success Metrics

Before moving to Phase 2 (AI scan), verify:

  • ✓ 5+ JB dentists onboarded from Dr Gerald intros
  • ✓ 20+ bookings generated to those dentists (proves OraChope value)
  • ✓ Positive feedback from intro'd dentists (they're happy with OraChope)
  • ✓ Dr Gerald sees clear benefit (his network is getting business)

Then propose Phase 2: "Dr Gerald, your dentists love OraChope. Now let's integrate your AI scan so users arrive with specific treatment needs already identified. This will boost conversions even more."

What to Propose to Dr Gerald

📧 Proposed Pitch

"Dr Gerald, we'd love to partner with OralLink. Here's what I'm thinking:

Phase 1 (Low Risk): Introduce us to 5 JB dentists from your network. We'll onboard them to OraChope and prove we can drive bookings. This costs you nothing but shows your network you're bringing them valuable business.

Phase 2 (After Proof): Once we've driven 20+ bookings to your dentist friends, we integrate OralLink AI scan into OraChope. We'll drive 100+ scans/month to your platform (validates your business model), and you continue introducing us to more dentists quarterly.

Win-Win: You get traffic + see your network benefit. We get JB dentist access + AI scan feature. Both parties start with minimal risk and scale based on results."

Technical Integration Approach (For Phase 2)

✓ Keep It Simple

  • Simple redirect: "Get AI Scan" button → orallink.health?ref=orachope
  • Scan ID pop-up: User saves ID before leaving (40-50% return rate accepted)
  • Manual return flow: User enters Scan ID when they come back
  • No iframe/nesting: Avoid complexity that doesn't improve results
  • Track performance: Monthly report to Dr Gerald on traffic volume
  • Iterate later: If ROI strong, invest in custom Kells redirect (Phase 3)

✗ Avoid These Traps

  • Don't over-engineer: No nesting, no complex iframe setup initially
  • Don't pay Kells upfront: Wait until proven partnership before custom redirect
  • Don't demand exclusivity: Keep options open for other partnerships
  • Don't ignore dead end problem: Be honest about 40-60% drop-off
  • Don't make AI scan the primary goal: Dentist network >> AI scan feature
