Implementation Notes - Option C Summary

Overall Pros:

  • Non-intrusive: doesn't interrupt conversation flow
  • Always accessible: user can click anytime
  • Clean UX: no forced messaging
  • Respects user autonomy

Overall Cons:

  • LOW DISCOVERY: Users may not notice button (banner blindness)
  • Passive approach = lower conversion rates
  • Misses users who need scan but don't realize it
  • No contextual nudging

Recommended IF:

You prioritize clean UX over conversion, already have scan CTA on homepage, or want to A/B test passive vs active approaches
