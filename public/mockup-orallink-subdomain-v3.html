How It Works

From sign-up to scan results in four simple steps

1

Start your free trial on OraChope

Create your Premium account — email and mobile only. No charge for 30 days.

2

Click "Start AI Dental Scan" on your dashboard

You will be securely redirected to OralLink's platform at scan.orallink.health to complete your 5-photo scan.

The 5 photos are: front teeth, left side, right side, upper jaw, lower jaw — taken with your phone camera in good lighting. No dentist or special equipment needed.

3

Receive your personalised dental assessment

OralLink's AI analyses your photos and returns a risk profile. Results are saved to your OraChope scan history.

4

Get matched to the right clinic on OraChope

Based on your results, OraChope recommends verified clinics in Singapore or JB suited to your needs and budget.
