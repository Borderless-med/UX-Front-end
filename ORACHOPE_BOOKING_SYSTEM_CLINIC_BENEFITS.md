## Why We Built This Booking System

Many dental clinics spend hours daily managing appointment bookings through phone calls and WhatsApp messages. Staff manually write down patient details, send confirmation messages one by one, and struggle to remind patients about upcoming appointments.

**The result?** 
- Staff time consumed by repetitive booking tasks
- Patients forget appointments (no-shows)
- Difficulty reaching patients to confirm or send reminders

OraChope.org's booking system solves these problems by **automating the entire booking workflow** - from initial request to appointment reminders - at **zero cost to clinics**.

---

## How the Booking System Works

### **Step 1: Patient Submits Booking Request**
- Patient fills out booking form on OraChope.org (name, phone, email, preferred date/time, treatment type)
- Patient gets **immediate confirmation** via email + WhatsApp with booking reference number

**What Clinic Receives:**
- **Email notification** with full patient details
- **WhatsApp notification** to clinic phone number
- **3 action buttons** to respond instantly:
  - ✅ **Confirm** - Accept the booking
  - ❌ **Reject** - Decline (with optional reason)
  - 🔄 **Offer Alternatives** - Suggest 3 different time slots

**No need to reply manually.** Just click the button that matches your decision.

---

### **Step 2: Clinic Responds (3 Hours to Respond)**

#### **Option A: Clinic Clicks "Confirm"**
- Patient immediately receives **confirmation email + WhatsApp** with:
  - Full clinic address
  - Google Maps link for directions
  - Travel guide link (for Singaporean patients visiting JB)
- Appointment is locked in - no further action needed

#### **Option B: Clinic Clicks "Reject"**
- Simple form appears asking for optional rejection reason
- Patient receives polite rejection notice
- Patient redirected to browse other clinics

#### **Option C: Clinic Clicks "Offer Alternatives"**
- Form appears where clinic enters 3-5 alternative time slots
- Patient receives email + WhatsApp with clickable slot options
- Patient clicks their preferred slot → Appointment confirmed
- Expiry time **automatically extended by 60 minutes** to give patient time to respond

#### **If Clinic Doesn't Respond:**
- **After 30 minutes:** System sends **urgent reminder** to clinic ("You have 30 minutes remaining to respond")
- **After 3 hours:** Booking automatically expires, patient notified

This ensures patients don't wait indefinitely for a response.

---

### **Step 3: Automated Appointment Reminder (24 Hours Before)**

**The system automatically sends:**
- **Email + WhatsApp reminder** to patient 24 hours before appointment
- Includes clinic address, Google Maps link, travel guide
- **No staff action required** - completely automated

**This reduces no-shows significantly** because patients receive a timely reminder without clinic staff needing to manually call or message each patient.

---

### **Step 4: Patient Cancellations**

If a patient needs to cancel:
- Patient clicks **"Cancel Appointment"** button in their confirmation email/WhatsApp
- Patient enters cancellation reason
- **Clinic immediately receives:**
  - **Email notification** with patient name, booking reference, date/time, and cancellation reason
  - **WhatsApp notification** with same details
- No action required from clinic - the slot is now available for other patients

## Common Questions

**Q: Do we need to install any software?**  
A: No. Everything works through email and WhatsApp notifications you already use daily.

**Q: What if we're busy and miss the 3-hour deadline?**  
A: You'll receive an urgent reminder after 30 minutes. If the booking expires, the patient is redirected to browse other clinics - no harm to your reputation.

**Q: Can we still use our existing booking system?**  
A: Yes! OraChope bookings are just **additional patients** coming from our platform. Your existing phone/WhatsApp bookings continue as normal.

**Q: How do we respond to bookings?**  
A: Simply click the button in the email or WhatsApp notification: "Confirm", "Reject", or "Offer Alternatives". Takes 10 seconds.

**Q: What if our WhatsApp number changes?**  
A: Contact us at contact@orachope.org and we'll update it immediately.

**Q: Is this really free forever?**  
A: Yes. We make our business sustainable through other means. Clinics never pay to use the booking system.

## Technical Details (For IT/Admin Staff)

### **Notifications Sent:**
- **Patient confirmation** (instant): Email + WhatsApp
- **Clinic alert** (instant): Email + WhatsApp with action buttons
- **Patient appointment confirmation** (when clinic confirms): Email + WhatsApp
- **24-hour reminder** (automated): Email + WhatsApp
- **Cancellation notice** (when patient cancels): Email + WhatsApp to clinic

### **Security:**
- All action links use **HMAC-SHA256 encryption** - cannot be forged or tampered
- Booking reference numbers are unique and trackable
- All data stored securely on Supabase (enterprise-grade database)

### **Reliability:**
- **99.9% uptime** on Vercel infrastructure
- **Automatic retry logic** - if a notification fails, system retries 3 times
- **Email backup** - if WhatsApp fails, email still delivers

### **Database:**
- JB clinics stored in `clinics_data` table
- SG clinics stored in `sg_clinics` table
- All bookings in `appointment_bookings` table with full audit trail
