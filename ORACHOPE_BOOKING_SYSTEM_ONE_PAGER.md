## The Problem

Dental clinic staff spend hours daily managing appointments through phone calls and WhatsApp. Patients forget appointments. Staff struggle to send reminders manually.

**Result:** Wasted staff time, frequent no-shows, missed follow-ups.

---

## The Solution: Automated Booking Workflow

### How It Works (4 Simple Steps)

**1. Patient Books**
- Patient fills form on OraChope.org → Gets instant confirmation via email + WhatsApp
- **Clinic receives:** Email + WhatsApp notification with 3 action buttons

**2. Clinic Responds (One-Click, 3-Hour Deadline)**
- ✅ **Confirm** → Patient notified instantly with Google Maps + directions
- ❌ **Reject** → Patient redirected to other clinics
- 🔄 **Offer Alternatives** → Suggest 3 slots, patient picks one
- **If no response:** Urgent reminder at 30 min, auto-expires at 3 hours

**3. Automated Reminder**
- System sends email + WhatsApp **24 hours before** appointment
- Includes address, Google Maps, travel guide
- **Zero staff effort**

**4. Cancellations**
- Patient clicks cancel button → Enters reason
- Clinic notified instantly via email + WhatsApp
